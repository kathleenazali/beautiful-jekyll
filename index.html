I'm currently based in Singapore as a Research Associate at the Institute of Southeast Asian Studies (ISEAS). As a researcher and practicing designer-developer, I am interested in the histories and intersections of information, (digital) technology, design, and society. My works on these issues have been presented/published in Networked Researcher Open Access (City University of London, 2012), Cornell University Southeast Asia Bulletin (2014), SOJOURN (2015, 2016), Oxford University (2016), University of Technology Sydney's Journal of Multidisciplinary International Studies (2016).

I founded C2O library & collabtive, an independent library and coworking community space in Surabaya, Indonesia, in 2008. I hold a Master in Cultural Studies from Airlangga University, with a thesis on alternative libraries, which was awarded 2013 Graduate Research Fellowship support from the Asia Research Institute, National University of Singapore.
